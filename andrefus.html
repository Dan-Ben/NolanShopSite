Andrefus range of shoes

First time in Ireland

Andrefus brand was born in 2006 in the Italy. The firm was founded in the 1960 by Gino Andrefus, inspired by his passion for making shoes by hand and stand out of unique design Gino Andrefus creativity and determination has transformed the company from a small craft workshop into a large footwear brand, sold in stores worldwide. Quality of materials, comfort and style are the main values of this company. Andrefus offers unique blend of style and comfort, mixing Italian footwear traditions with technical innovations. This firm produces boots and shoes which are sold in more than 20 countries. The ancient traditonal method of manufacture, the use of the finest materials, combined with the elegance, and the passion are the main characterise of Andrefus brand. And first time it will be available as well for Irelands market from Nolan Shoe store at Graffton street.
